Safer transit — quick reminders

  • Share your route and expected arrival time with one trusted person before you travel, especially for night shifts.
  • Keep the vehicle number of any cab, auto or shared transport you take, and send it to someone before you get in.
  • If a workplace requires late hours, ask in writing for transport or a group departure — it is a reasonable request, not a favour.
  • Trust a bad feeling about a route, driver or space. Changing plans costs a few minutes; ignoring the feeling can cost more.
